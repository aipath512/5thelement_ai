AI Authority™ · Signals Layer

AI SIGNALS™

HELP · €49/hour · Delivered in 1 week
Verifiable™

AI SIGNALS™ optimizes the atomic machine-readable signals that help AI systems classify, verify, and connect your business across the web and AI knowledge graphs.

This is the layer that turns a readable business into a verifiable one. AI systems can now cross-reference your claims, confirm consistency across sources, and weight your data with higher confidence.

167 signals across three categories: Identity & Entity, Trust & Proof, Intent & Governance. Deployed via Cloudflare Workers KV.

AI Authority™ · Full Implementation

Trust Authority Module™

Done For You · Evaluation Required
Recommendable™

Trust Authority Module™ is the full authority enforcement layer. We build the cryptographic proof layer, establish cross-references, deploy citations, and create an immutable evidence layer that makes AI confident in recommending your business over competitors.
