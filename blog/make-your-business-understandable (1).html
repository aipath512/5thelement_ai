How to Make Your Business Understandable by AI

What Does Understandable™ Mean?

Understandable™ is Stage 2 of the AI Visibility Ladder. It means AI systems correctly understand what the business does, who it serves, what problems it solves, and what services it offers.

This stage builds directly on the previous stage. Each stage in the AI Visibility Ladder is a prerequisite for the next — you cannot skip to Understandable™ without first achieving the stages below it.

Understandable™ is not a destination — it is a threshold. Once crossed, it must be maintained through continuous signal health monitoring and regular authority layer updates.

What Understandable™ Requires

Service schema, FAQPage markup, service-to-intent mapping, buyer persona signals, and problem-solution structured data

The exact signals required depend on the business's current position on the AI Visibility Ladder. AI AUDIT™ produces a precise gap matrix showing exactly which signals are missing, inconsistent, or unverifiable — mapped to this specific stage.

How to Achieve Understandable™

01

Measure current position

Run AI AUDIT™ to establish baseline score and identify every gap blocking Understandable™ status. The gap matrix is stage-specific — it shows exactly which signals are missing for this stage.

02

Build the required signals

Use AI LENS™ to acquire all business knowledge needed for the stage-specific signals. Then use AI READY™ to generate the structured files and AI SIGNALS™ to deploy them.

03

Deploy to the edge

All new signal files are deployed via EDGE INJECTOR™ to the Cloudflare edge — available to AI crawlers globally in under 10ms without touching the existing website.

04

Verify across all five AI systems

Ask each AI system: can you tell me what services Company X offers and who they serve? Correct, complete answers indicate Stage 2. AI SONAR™ automates this verification weekly and alerts immediately when status drops below the threshold.

Maintaining Understandable™ Status

Understandable™ status is not permanent. Signal decay, competitor authority gains, and AI system knowledge graph updates can all reduce visibility. AI SONAR™ monitors Understandable™ status continuously across all five AI systems and alerts when corrective action is needed.
