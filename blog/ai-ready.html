AI READY™
Fix What AI Cannot Read.

AI READY™ is the Automatic Transformation Engine. It takes the structured knowledge from AI LENS™ and transforms it into every structured file, schema, and content format that AI systems require — without touching your existing website, CMS, or hosting infrastructure.

The Problem

Knowing What Is Missing Is Not the Same as Fixing It

AI LENS™ delivers the gap list. AI AUDIT™ delivers the score. But neither of these makes the business more visible to AI. That requires implementation — and implementation is where most businesses stall.

The traditional path requires a developer, a content writer, a schema specialist, and a project manager. Weeks of work. High cost. High coordination overhead. AI READY™ removes all of this. It implements the fixes automatically — from the Business Knowledge Graph™ — without any manual content creation, without any developer involvement, and without touching the existing website.

Five Generation Types

What AI READY™ Generates

Every generation type is derived from the Business Knowledge Graph™. Nothing invented. Everything verified before output.

01

Content Generation

Service descriptions, entity statements, FAQ content, team profiles, and location descriptions — written in the declarative, AI-parseable format that produces the highest AI visibility impact. Not narrative content for human persuasion. Structured declarative content that serves both humans and AI systems simultaneously.

Every generated content item is grounded in the Business Knowledge Graph™. Nothing is invented. Everything derived from verified business information. Human review mandatory before deployment.

02

Schema Generation

Complete Schema.org markup for every page type — Organization on the homepage, Service on every service page, FAQPage on FAQ sections, BreadcrumbList on every page, and sector-specific schema types where applicable.

Every schema validated against the Schema.org specification and against the specific requirements of each major AI system before output. Common errors — missing required properties, incorrect types, conflicting markup — identified and corrected automatically.

Organization JSON-LD Service JSON-LD FAQPage JSON-LD BreadcrumbList JSON-LD
03

FAQ Generation

Structured question-and-answer pairs mapped directly to real buyer queries. Every FAQ entry derived from the Intent Registry — the question from buyer query patterns, the answer from the Business Knowledge Graph™.

Delivered in two formats: human-readable HTML content for the FAQ page, and FAQPage JSON-LD schema for the page head. AI systems can cite FAQ answers directly — without visiting the page — when the schema is correctly deployed.

04

Entity Graph Generation

The complete entity graph in JSON-LD format — ready for deployment at the domain root and edge endpoint. Every entity correctly typed, every relationship directional, every identifier stable, and every sameAs reference validated before output.

entities.json entity-graph.public.jsonld
05

AI Files Generation

The primary AI discovery files generated from the Business Knowledge Graph™. Every file validated before output. Every file version-stamped and ready for SHA-256 fingerprinting by PROOF LAYER™.

ai.json llms.txt robots.txt intents.json
The Transformation

Concrete. Measurable. Verifiable.

Every gap identified in AI LENS™ and ranked in AI AUDIT™ is addressed. The before/after is concrete.

Before AI READY™
  • Narrative service descriptions on HTML pages
  • No schema markup on any page
  • No machine-readable entity data
  • AI crawlers blocked in robots.txt
  • No llms.txt at domain root
  • No ai.json deployed
  • AI cannot describe the business accurately
  • FAQ exists but not structured as schema
→
After AI READY™
  • Declarative service descriptions with Service schema
  • Organization schema on homepage
  • FAQPage schema on FAQ sections
  • All AI crawlers explicitly permitted
  • llms.txt deployed at domain root
  • ai.json live — entity defined
  • AI describes business accurately and completely
  • FAQ deployed as FAQPage — AI can cite directly
What Changes. What Does Not.

Zero Website Risk. Full AI Transformation.

What Gets Added

  • JSON-LD script tags in page heads (invisible to visitors)
  • robots.txt updated — AI crawlers permitted
  • ai.json at domain root
  • llms.txt at domain root
  • entities.json at domain root
  • intents.json at domain root
  • New FAQ page with FAQPage schema

What Is Never Touched

  • Website design or layout
  • CMS configuration or plugins
  • Existing page content or copy
  • Hosting configuration
  • Database or backend systems
  • Any existing HTML, CSS, or JavaScript
  • Content management workflow

The Process

Five Steps. From Gap List to Deployed Files.

01

Input — Business Knowledge Graph™

AI READY™ consumes the Business Knowledge Graph™ from AI LENS™ and the Gap Registry from AI AUDIT™. No additional input required from the business.

02

Automated Generation

All five generation types run automatically. Content, schema, FAQ, entity graph, and AI files generated from verified knowledge graph data. No manual writing or schema coding required.

03

Validation

Every generated item validated. Schema checked against Schema.org specification. sameAs references verified. AI file formats validated. No invalid output is passed to the next step.

04

Human Review — HITL Checkpoint

Every generated item reviewed by a human analyst. Content accuracy confirmed. Schema completeness verified. Nothing deployed without human sign-off.

05

Delivery for Deployment

All generated files packaged for deployment. Ready for direct deployment or EDGE INJECTOR™ deployment. AI AUDIT™ post-implementation score verification included.
