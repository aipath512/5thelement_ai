The Problem

AI Cannot Understand What Has Never Been Structured

AI systems do not understand businesses the way humans do. A human reading a website can infer context, fill in gaps, and make reasonable assumptions. AI systems cannot. They rely on explicit, structured, machine-readable data.

The first question is not how to fix visibility. The first question is what information exists, what is missing, and what must be created before any signal can be deployed. AI LENS™ answers this question. It is the diagnostic and knowledge-building engine that every other product in the platform depends on.

What It Does

Five Core Functions

AI LENS™ performs a complete business knowledge acquisition across five structured functions.

Information Inventory

Catalogues all existing business information — website content, documents, service brochures, team profiles, case studies, certifications, social profiles, and directory listings. Every piece assessed for accuracy, completeness, machine-readability, and AI-visibility relevance.

Entity and Relationship Mapping

Every identified entity is mapped — the business, every service, every team member, every location, every certification, every award, every client type, every partner, and every competitor. Relationships typed and directional.

Gap Identification

Every piece of information AI systems need but that does not yet exist in structured form is logged in the Gap Registry. Missing service descriptions, absent founding date, no intent mapping — all gaps ranked by impact and referenced to the downstream fix.

AI Visibility Information Map™

The primary output. A structured visual representation of everything AI can and cannot find about the business — across 100 dimensions, seven categories, with green (present), red (missing), and amber (inconsistent) status for each dimension.

Business Knowledge Graph™

The canonical structured representation of the business as AI systems need to understand it. The primary source of truth for all downstream products — AI READY™, SIGNAL COMPILER™, KNOWLEDGE GRAPH™, and TRUST LAYER™.

Intent Registry

A structured map of all buyer intents the business should appear for — the queries that trigger them, the services that satisfy them, and the negative intents it should be excluded from. Input for AI SIGNALS™ and KNOWLEDGE GRAPH™.

The Process

Six Steps. 24 Hours.

01

Input Collection

Business provides website URL, primary service category, target geography, and any existing documents. No credentials required.

02

Automated Extraction

AI LENS™ extracts all machine-readable information from the website and documents. Entity candidates identified. Service descriptions parsed. Certifications catalogued.

03

Structured Mapping

Every extracted piece of information structured into the entity and relationship model. Every gap identified against the 100-dimension framework.

04

Human Review

Every extraction reviewed by a human analyst. Ambiguous classifications resolved. Missing information flagged for creation. HITL checkpoint — nothing invented.

05

Output Generation

All five output files generated. Business Knowledge Graph™ built. AI Visibility Information Map™ produced. Gap Registry finalised.

06

Delivery and Debrief

All outputs delivered within 24 hours. Optional 30-minute debrief call to walk through findings and recommended next actions.
