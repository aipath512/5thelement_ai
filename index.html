FAQ

Six questions.
Honest answers.

Do I need to change my website?+
No. We add an AI visibility layer alongside your existing website — not inside it. Your site, CMS, hosting, and design stay exactly as they are. We work at the network edge.
How is this different from SEO?+
SEO optimizes for search engine ranking algorithms. AI Visibility targets how AI systems understand, verify, and recommend your business in generated answers. Different systems, different signals, different outcomes.
How fast will I see results?+
Free AI Lens audit delivered within 24 hours. After implementation, recommendation improvement in AI systems is typically measurable within 2–4 weeks. We provide before-and-after verification so you can test directly.
What if AI still does not recommend me?+
We provide a result-based guarantee. If the agreed improvements don't produce measurable change in how AI systems understand your business, we continue working until they do. No AI system can be forced to recommend anyone — but we guarantee the work.
Is this compliant with the EU AI Act?+
Our work supports EU AI Act readiness. We never certify compliance — that distinction is fundamental to our epistemic discipline. We help businesses document, structure, and verify their AI-related claims in line with Article 50 transparency requirements.
What types of businesses does this work for?+
Any B2B company that wants to be found, understood, and recommended by AI systems. Professional services, technology companies, consultancies, agencies, and any business where B2B buyers use AI to evaluate options before making contact.
