Market Shift

The Way Customers Buy Is Changing

The shift is not that websites disappear. The shift is not that Google disappears. The shift is that the buyer’s representative changes.

Before, the human searched, compared, trusted, clicked, contacted and bought. Now, AI searches, compares, verifies, filters, recommends and may soon buy.

Old road: human search through Google, ads, SEO and social media. New road: AI agent searches, compares, verifies, recommends and routes the customer toward selected options.

Traffic Economy

Customer → Google → Website → Contact → Purchase

The customer performs the work: search, comparison, evaluation, contact and buying decision.

Answer Economy

Customer → AI → Answer → Recommendation

AI performs part of the work: searches, compares, summarizes, explains and recommends.

Recommendation Economy

Customer → AI Agent → Verified Options → Purchase

AI agents search, explore, compare, verify, filter, recommend, select and increasingly buy.

For the first time, companies are not selling only to people. They are also selling to the machines that represent people.

That changes marketing. That changes sales. That changes growth.
