The Problem

AI Visibility Is Not a Destination. It Is a Dynamic State.

AI systems continuously update their knowledge graphs, re-index content, adjust weighting algorithms, and respond to new information from competitors, industry sources, and user feedback patterns. A business that achieves Recommendable™ status today is not guaranteed to maintain it tomorrow.

Signals decay when they are not maintained. Competitors strengthen their authority layers. New AI systems enter the market. Events — a name change, a new competitor, a regulatory development — can alter how AI systems classify and recommend a business without any action on the business's part.

AI SONAR™ is the monitoring layer that ensures a business always knows exactly where it stands — in real time, across every major AI system, against every relevant competitor.

What It Monitors

Six Monitor Types. Continuous Coverage.

Each monitor type targets a specific aspect of AI visibility. Together they provide complete situational awareness.

01

Mentions

Every time the business is mentioned in an AI-generated answer — across all five systems. Records the query that triggered the mention, the context, the accuracy of the description, and the sentiment. Distinguishes between primary recommendation, secondary mention, and comparison mention.

Frequency: Continuous · 24/7
02

Rankings

For every target buyer query in the Intent Registry, tracks the business's current position in AI recommendation responses — first, second, third, or absent. Week-over-week trend with directional indicator. Competitor ranking positions tracked on the same queries for direct comparison.

Frequency: Weekly per query · Daily for high-priority queries
03

Visibility Score

Maintains a continuous AI Visibility Score — updated weekly — reflecting the business's current AI visibility across all five stages of the AI Visibility Ladder from Findable™ to Recommendable™. Benchmarked against the baseline from the most recent AI AUDIT™ and trended over time with week, month, quarter, and year views.

Frequency: Weekly update · Monthly trend analysis
04

Competitor Monitoring

Monitors the top three competitors on the same target queries. Tracks their mention frequency, ranking positions, description accuracy, and any changes to their signal layer or authority profile. When a competitor strengthens their position or deploys new signals, AI SONAR™ detects this and flags it as a competitive event requiring strategic response.

Frequency: Weekly · Monthly competitive intelligence report
05

Signal Health

Monitors the health of all 167 deployed signals. Checks every signal file for accessibility, consistency, and currency. Detects signal decay — where a previously deployed signal has become inaccessible, inconsistent, or outdated — and triggers an alert before the decay impacts the visibility score.

Frequency: Daily health check · Immediate alert on anomaly
06

Description Accuracy

Evaluates whether AI systems are describing the business correctly — correct name, correct category, correct services, correct location. Inaccuracies are flagged and traced to the specific signal gap or inconsistency causing the error. Correction recommendations are included in the next weekly report.

Frequency: Weekly · Immediate alert on significant inaccuracy
Alert System

Immediate Alerts for Every Significant Change

Do not wait for the weekly report to learn about critical changes. AI SONAR™ alerts immediately when it matters.

📉

Visibility Score Drop

Immediate alert when AI Visibility Score drops 5 or more points in a single week. Includes dimensional breakdown identifying which layer is causing the decline.

🚨

Signal File Inaccessibility

Immediate alert when any signal file becomes inaccessible to AI crawlers — within hours of the failure occurring. Includes affected file and recommended corrective action.

⚔️

Competitor Mention Surge

Alert when a competitor's mention frequency for shared target queries increases by 20% or more in a single week — indicating a significant authority signal deployment.

❌

Description Accuracy Degradation

Alert when AI systems begin describing the business incorrectly in two or more monitored query responses — indicating a knowledge graph conflict requiring immediate resolution.

🆕

New AI System Entry

Alert when a new AI system enters the market that requires signal format updates or crawler permission additions — with recommended technical actions.

📊

Ranking Loss

Alert when the business drops out of the top three recommendation positions for any high-priority target query — with competitive analysis of which business has taken the position.

The Monitoring Methodology

How AI SONAR™ Works

01

Target Query Set Construction

All target buyer queries are drawn from the Intent Registry built by AI LENS™. Queries cover direct recommendation requests, comparison requests, category searches, location-filtered searches, and problem-based searches across all target markets and languages.

02

Structured Query Execution

Every target query is run on a defined schedule — daily for high-priority queries, weekly for secondary queries — across all five AI systems. Results are captured, structured, and stored in the monitoring database.

03

Expected vs Actual Comparison

Results are evaluated against the expected mention, expected description, and expected ranking position defined in the AI Visibility baseline from the most recent AI AUDIT™. Every deviation is classified and severity-scored.

04

Anomaly Classification

Every anomaly — unexpected mention, unexpected absence, unexpected competitor gain, description inaccuracy, signal health failure — is classified by type, severity, and likely cause. Classification determines alert priority and corrective action.

05

Report and Alert Generation

Alerts are dispatched immediately for critical anomalies. Weekly reports are compiled from all monitoring data and dispatched every Monday. Monthly competitor intelligence reports are compiled from weekly data and dispatched on the first Monday of each month.
