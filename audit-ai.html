§ 05 — Questions

Everything you
need to know.

Is the audit really free?
Yes. The free audit runs 167 checks, scores your AI visibility across 5 dimensions, tests whether you are cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity and Claude, and gives you your top 10 priority fixes. No account, no credit card, no time limit. The free tier is free forever.
How long does the audit take?
The automated audit completes in under 3 minutes. Our agentic engine queries 5 AI platforms and scans 167 signals simultaneously. You enter your domain, wait briefly, and receive your full report.
What do you need from me to run the audit?
Just your domain name and your primary business category. Nothing else. We do not require access to your CMS, hosting, analytics or any internal systems.
My website is modern and well-optimised for Google. Will I still score low?
Very likely, yes. Google SEO and AI visibility are fundamentally different. Google indexes pages. AI engines parse structured entities, verify claims through proof signals, and require machine-readable intent mapping. Most websites — even excellent ones — score below 4/10 on AI visibility because they were never built for AI consumption.
How is your audit different from other AI visibility tools?
Most tools check whether you appear in AI answers — a surface-level observation. Our audit goes deeper: we check the 167 underlying signals that determine whether AI will ever cite you. We also include EU AI Act compliance scoring, cryptographic proof verification, and a competitor gap analysis — none of which other tools currently offer.
After the audit, what happens next?
You receive your score and priority fix list. From there you choose your path: implement the fixes yourself using our guide (free), work with us in guided sessions (€49/h), or have us deploy everything for you (from €2,500). The audit tells you what to do. The path you choose determines how fast it happens.
