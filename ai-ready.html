§ 03 — What We Build

167 signals.
8 core files.

Every AI-Ready deployment includes a complete set of machine-readable signal files — the exact files AI crawlers parse when building their knowledge of your business.

ai.json
Primary AI discovery file. Declares your entity, services, authority signals and ADI™ proof layer.
entities.json
Structured entity definition. Business name, type, location, founding date, identifiers — unambiguous identity for AI models.
intents.json
Multilingual intent mapping (EN/FR/RO/DE). Maps buyer questions to your services across 6 funnel stages.
ai-proof.json
Cryptographic proof layer. SHA-256 hashes, OpenTimestamps anchors, Bitcoin block references. Machine-verifiable authority.
llms.txt
LLM discovery file. Tells AI crawlers what your business does, what to cite, and what permissions apply.
schema markup
25+ Schema.org types injected: Organization, Service, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, Product, Review and more.
robots.txt
AI crawler access configuration. Explicit allow-lanes for ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini and other AI bots.
sitemap.xml
AI-optimised sitemap with priority signals, last-modified dates and content-type annotations for machine parsing.

§ 06 — Questions

Everything you
need to know.

Will this break my existing website?
No. The ADI™ layer is deployed at the Cloudflare network edge — completely separate from your existing site infrastructure. Your WordPress, Webflow, or custom site remains 100% unchanged. We add signals above your site, not inside it.
How is this different from SEO?
SEO optimises your pages for Google's crawler. AI-Ready optimises your signals for AI language models. Google indexes pages. AI engines parse structured entities, verify claims through proof signals, and require machine-readable intent mapping. They are completely different systems requiring completely different approaches.
How long until I appear in AI answers?
Deployment completes within 24 hours. AI engines typically begin updating their indexes within 24–72 hours of your signals going live. Citation frequency increases progressively over the first 2–4 weeks as AI models re-crawl and update their knowledge of your business.
Do I need a developer?
For the DIY path: basic technical comfort is enough — if you can set up a CNAME record and push files to GitHub, you can deploy ADI™ yourself using our guide. For Guided support: we walk you through every step. For Done For You: you need nothing — we handle everything from build to deployment.
What is the SHA-256 anchoring and Bitcoin timestamping?
Every signal file we create is fingerprinted with a SHA-256 hash — a mathematical proof that the file's content is exactly as published. That hash is then anchored to the Bitcoin blockchain via OpenTimestamps, giving it an immutable, decentralised timestamp. AI engines can verify both the integrity of your data and the date it was established — making your authority claims machine-verifiable rather than just asserted.
Does AI-Ready include EU AI Act compliance?
Yes. Every Done For You deployment includes an EU AI Act Article 50 compliance layer — AI disclosure notices, chatbot identification, AI-generated content labelling, and privacy policy AI clauses. The Guided path includes compliance review. The DIY guide covers the basic requirements you need to implement yourself.
