The Core Problem

It is not about being bad. It is about being invisible or unverifiable to AI systems.

"Who are the best accounting firms in Bucharest?"

Problem 1 — Not Findable

AI cannot locate your business in its knowledge graph. Your name, services, location, and expertise are not machine-readable. You do not appear in the answer — even if you are the best firm in the city.

AI can find your business but cannot verify your claims. Your proof layer is missing, inconsistent, or unverifiable. AI recommends a competitor with stronger authority signals — even if your service is superior.
