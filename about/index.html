30 Years of Enterprise Architecture. One New Category.

Dan Ionescu is a French-Romanian enterprise architect with over 30 years of experience in large-scale IT infrastructure — including IBM France, CGI Informatique, and CICS/COBOL/mainframe systems at enterprise scale.

In March 2025, Dan founded AiVenture S.R.L. in Bucharest with a single thesis: AI visibility is not a ranking game — it is a graph-confidence game. The company that structures its information correctly, proves its claims verifiably, and deploys the right machine-readable signals will be recommended by AI. Everyone else will be invisible.

5thelement.ai is the result of applying enterprise architecture discipline — deterministic pipelines, structured data, cryptographic proof, and edge deployment — to the problem of AI visibility for B2B companies. The methodology is the same. The target is different. The opportunity is 12 million EU businesses that are currently invisible to AI.

Dan signs as Ionesco in French contexts and Ionescu in Romanian and English contexts. Both are intentional and correct.
