── WHAT IS llms.txt ──

llms.txt is a plain-text file placed at the root of a website.

Example:

https://yourdomain.com/llms.txt

It helps AI systems understand:

Who you are

What you do

What files matter

How AI should interact with your content

Think of it as:

robots.txt = instructions for crawlers

llms.txt = instructions for AI

── WHY IT EXISTS ──

Websites were originally built for humans.

AI systems need structured context.

Without llms.txt AI must guess.

With llms.txt AI receives direct instructions.

This improves:

Understanding

Classification

Retrieval

Citation

Consistency

── FAQ ──

1. Is llms.txt an official standard?

No.

It is an emerging convention.

2. Is it required?

Not officially.

Strongly recommended.

3. Does it replace robots.txt?

No.

They serve different purposes.

4. Does it replace ai.json?

No.

Use both.

5. Where should it be deployed?

At the root of the domain.

Example:

https://yourdomain.com/llms.txt
