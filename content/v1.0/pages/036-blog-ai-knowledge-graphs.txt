── WHAT IS A KNOWLEDGE GRAPH ──

A Knowledge Graph is a network of entities and relationships.

AI systems do not think in pages.

AI systems think in entities.

Examples of entities:

Business

Person

Service

Product

Location

Organization

Certification

Client

Partner

Each entity is connected to other entities.

These connections help AI understand reality.

── SIMPLE EXAMPLE ──

AiVenture

↓

provides

↓

AI Visibility™

↓

delivered through

↓

AI READY™

↓

used by

↓

B2B Companies

This is a graph.

Not a webpage.

Not a keyword.

A relationship.

── WHY KNOWLEDGE GRAPHS MATTER ──

Without a graph:

AI sees isolated information.

With a graph:

AI understands connections.

This improves:

Understanding

Verification

Trust

Recommendations

Selection

── THE THREE CORE ELEMENTS ──

ELEMENT 1 — ENTITIES

Things that exist.

Examples:

Company

Person

Service

Location

Product

Certification

---

ELEMENT 2 — RELATIONSHIPS

Connections between entities.

Examples:

provides

owns

worksFor

locatedIn

certifiedBy

partnerOf

createdBy

---

ELEMENT 3 — PROPERTIES

Facts about entities.

Examples:

Name

Date

Category

Address

Language

Registration Number

URL
