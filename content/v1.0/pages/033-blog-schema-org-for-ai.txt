── WHAT IS SCHEMA.ORG ──

Schema.org is the world's most widely used structured data vocabulary.

It helps machines understand information.

Humans read pages.

Machines read schema.

AI systems use Schema.org to understand:

Businesses

People

Services

Products

Articles

Questions

Locations

Relationships

Schema is normally written as JSON-LD.

── WHY IT MATTERS FOR AI ──

Without Schema.org:

AI guesses.

With Schema.org:

AI receives explicit facts.

Benefits:

Better understanding

Better classification

Better verification

Better entity matching

Higher confidence

Improved recommendation potential
