── WHAT THEY ARE ──

Identity and Entity Signals are the 48 atomic data points that establish
who a business is inside AI knowledge graphs.

This is the prerequisite category.

AI cannot trust,
verify,
or recommend
an entity it cannot identify.

All other signal categories depend on these first.
