── WHAT IS ai.json ──

ai.json is the primary machine-readable identity file for a business.

It acts as the central AI identity document.

Location:

https://yourdomain.com/ai.json

AI systems can use it to understand:

Who the business is

What the business does

Where the business operates

What services it offers

How the business connects to other entities

Think of ai.json as:

A digital business passport for AI.

── WHY ai.json EXISTS ──

Most websites are built for humans.

AI systems need structured data.

Without ai.json:

AI must guess.

With ai.json:

AI receives structured facts.

This improves:

Discovery

Understanding

Verification

Trust

Recommendation potential
