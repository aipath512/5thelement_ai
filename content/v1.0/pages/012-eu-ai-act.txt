── WHAT IS THE EU AI ACT? ──

The EU AI Act is the world's first comprehensive legal framework
governing artificial intelligence systems.

Entry into force:

1 August 2024

Objective:

Create a common framework for safe,
transparent,
and accountable AI systems across the European Union.

── KEY DATES ──

2 February 2025

Prohibited AI practices become applicable.

---

2 August 2025

General Purpose AI (GPAI) obligations become applicable.

---

2 August 2026

High-risk AI system obligations become applicable.

---

2 August 2027

Full application including Annex I systems.
